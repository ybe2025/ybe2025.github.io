Combinatorial Approaches to the Yang–Baxter Equation

Celebrating the Appointment of Dr. Ilaria Colazzo

Date: 9 October 2025
Location: University of Leeds (UK)

Overview & Objectives

This one-day workshop brings together researchers working on the combinatorial aspects of the Yang–Baxter equation and related algebraic structures.

  • 12:30–14:00 — Lunch

Afternoon Session

  • 14:00–15:00 — Anastasia Doiku (Heriot-Watt University)
  • 15:00–16:00 — Eric Jespers (Vrije Universiteit Brussel)
  • 16:00–17:30 — Reception

Speakers & Participants

The conference includes senior researchers and invited speakers with expertise in algebra, combinatorics, and mathematical physics. There will be short presentations from PhD students and postdocs during the Gong Talks session.
